# Product Hunt Tagline

PH tagline limit: **60 characters** (including spaces). Counted below each option.

Pick one. Rank the others as backups in case the first feels off after sleeping on it.

---

## Option 1 — Feature-forward

> **Markdown editor, terminal, and browser for AI-native dev**

Chars: 53/60

Pros: tells someone scanning the homepage exactly what the app is. Unambiguous category placement.
Cons: reads like every other dev-tool tagline. Doesn't filter.

---

## Option 2 — Anti-marketing filter (Recommended)

> **The markdown editor that knows what a CLAUDE.md is**

Chars: 51/60

Pros: matches the `launch-strategy.md` one-liner. Filters in the right audience, filters out the rest. Memorable.
Cons: requires PH audience to know what CLAUDE.md is. Many will — they hunt developer tools. Some won't scroll past it.

---

## Option 3 — Workflow framing

> **Wrangle your agents — native macOS workspace for AI dev**

Chars: 55/60

Pros: uses the product name as a verb (matches "wrangle your agents" tagline on landing page). Ties the name to an action.
Cons: "wrangle your agents" is slightly cute. Works on a website with visual support; a cold PH scroller might miss the payoff.

---

## Recommendation

**Go with Option 2** for Product Hunt specifically. PH audiences over-index on developers, and the filter-in is an asset — the people who nod at CLAUDE.md are exactly the ~10k target users. Everyone else scrolls past and that's fine.

If post-launch analytics show PH traffic was too narrow, swap to Option 1 for the retained listing page (you can edit your PH listing after launch day).
